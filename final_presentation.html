BirdCast Migration - Data Visualization

Data Introduction

BirdCast is a subset of the Cornell Lab of Ornithology, along with other websites and apps you may have already heard of like Merlin or eBird. BirdCast uses Doppler weather radar to detect the number of migrating birds during the migration season to support bird conservation and expand knowledge of migratory bird movement.

  • Active from March 1 to June 15 and August 1 to November 15; I am specifically looking at March 1, 2026 to the present.
  • I scraped data on number of birds, flight speed, and species.
  • I also scraped data from NOAA to compare bird migration to weather variables.

Question(s) of Interest

  • What does nightly bird migration look like in three different counties across the East Coast?
  • How do weather variables like precipitation and temperature impact bird migration?
